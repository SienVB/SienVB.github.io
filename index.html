My name is Sien Van Broekhoven and currently I'm studying Cloud & Cyber Security (Electronics - ICT) at the Thomas More university in Geel, Belgium. At the moment I'm in my last year of the 3 year long bachelor education. I've recently completed an internship at Hewlett Packard Enterprise, read more about this internship here.

Why Cloud & Cyber Security?

Informatics and technology have always interested me a lot, so after completing my high school education of Accountancy/ICT I was very motivated to further my studies in computer science. After a first year of getting to know some of the aspects of computer science: programming, networking, and IOT; I decided to continue my studies in the networking area, so Cloud and Cyber Security. The aspects of networking, cloud/hybrid infrastructure, and security are the most interesting to me.
